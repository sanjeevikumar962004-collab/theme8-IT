Common Questions

What is your typical development process?

Our process is highly Agile-driven. We start with discovery and UI/UX design, move into sprints during development, and finish with rigorous QA testing before deployment.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Absolutely. We provide dedicated post-launch support packages, including server maintenance, security patching, and scaling as your business grows.

How do you ensure the security of the software?

Security is integrated from day one. We employ industry best practices, conduct regular code reviews, penetration testing, and use robust encryption standards to protect your data.

Can you work with our existing technical team?

Yes! Under our "Dedicated Team" engagement model, our engineers can seamlessly integrate into your existing workflow, using your tools and communication platforms.
